‘Document Details’

 

The document details are displayed on this page.

  • Click the relevant Edit buttons to edit the document name or add Generic Meta Data (may not be available)
  • Click the Download button to download a copy of this document onto your PC
  • Click the Email button to e-mail the document link to another user
  • Click the Delete button to delete this document (may not be available)
  • Click the History button to view the transaction history of this document.
  • Click the Check-Out button to check this document out, or the Check-In button to check the document back in. Note: Other users cannot make updates to a checked-out document.
  • Click the Subscribe/ Unsubscribe button to change your subscription status. Subscribing to this document means that you will receive notifications of any changes to the status of the document, such as check-outs or updates. Clicking the Unsubscribe button will stop the notifications from being sent to you.

 

For authorised users only:

This page displays the document routing details. These include the collaboration sequence and whether or not this step of the collaboration process is Active (pending approval) or Done (approved).

To assign a player to a particular role, click the Edit button next to the role you wish to assign.

Once all the players have been assigned click Begin to start the collaboration process. An immediate notification will be sent to the first player in the sequence.
